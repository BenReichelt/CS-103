A look at Kapa'a:

One of Earth's hidden beauties

Introduction:

After my Sophomore year in college, I took a trip to Hawaii and visited what quickly became one of my favorite places on Earth, Kauai. I was enthralled with the natural beauty of the landscapes, wildlife, and environment of the island. Here are a few pictures showing some examples of the island's beauty by showcasing the city of Kapa'a.

While hiking and exploring, it is common to see natural features that give you a full sense of appreciation for nature, as well as the effect of the passage of time on the environment.

It is common to see chickens and roosters roaming the streets of Kapa'a similarly to how squirrels and pigeons roam the city of Boston. The chickens have free reign because a natural predator was never introduced to the island, hence Kauai's nickname of "Chicken Island".
